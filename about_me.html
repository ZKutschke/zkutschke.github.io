My personal projects take a lot of different forms depending on what grabbed my interest at that particular moment and what resources I had available
This site mainly consists of the engineering-type projects I've undertaken in recent(ish) years. I do have to apologize for the overall quality of the site, my main focus is on getting the content up rather than building a beautiful website. But please feel free to take a look at what I've worked on and to reach out with any questions.
